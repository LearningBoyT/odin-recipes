Wooden pickaxe

Description

This is the minecraft crafting recipe for a wooden pickaxe.

Warning:do not try this in real life.

Ingredients

  • 3 wooden planks
  • 2 sticks

Steps

  1. Put the planks along the top of your crafting table.
  2. Put one stick in the middle and one in the bottom middle.
  3. Congratulations, now you have a wooden pickaxe!
